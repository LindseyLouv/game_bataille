Rules

  • A deck of 52 cards has been shuffled and dealt equally between players.
  • Each turn, player will play 1 card, the player with the biggest card takes the oponent's card.
  • Click on Play to play a card.
  • Click on End Game to finish the game in which the next win will determine the Winner.
